This track aims to attract papers presenting research and development results related to High Performance Computing, Grid Computing, Parallel Computation, Cloud Computing and Networking. Submissions should be original and present previously unpublished advances in academia and in the industry. The areas of interest for the track include, but are not limited to, parallel architectures, parallel and distributed software, performance in communications and networking using HPC techniques, distributed and parallel algorithms, green computing, and aspects related to Cloud Computing such as architectures, models, efficiency, and security, among others.
