CIbSE 2018 organizes a Doctoral Symposium aimed at disseminating research-in-progress conducted by novice researchers. A board of experts will discuss with the researchers to suggest new perspectives and improvements to the PhD thesis.

The submitted research can address any of the CIbSE 2018 research topics. We recommend, although it is not compulsory, that the research reached an intermediate stage (~ 2º thesis year), so that experts’ suggestions could be included in the PhD work.
