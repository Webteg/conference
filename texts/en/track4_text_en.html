The industry presentations track at CIbSE looks to bring closer software engineering researchers and industry practitioners to exchange ideas, learn about each other's ideas, and foster colaboration.

The industry track looks to highlight modern practices, problems, and solutions in the industry to generate a better understanding for academics about the current industrial mindset and future challenges. This, with the objective of fostering a close colaboration between the responsibles for the concepts and tools used in software development, and the practitioners using such tools in their everyday life. This way we can build the next generation of practices and tools together!
