The Universidad de los Andes consists of many buildings spread over “El eje ambiental”. In particular, the conference venue is the “Mario Laserna” building located in Carrera 1 Este # 19 A - 40 (Bogota), this place is also known as the “Engineering” building. You can reach this place by taxi or public transport. The Transmilenio bus stations closer to the venue are “Universidades” and “Las Aguas”.
