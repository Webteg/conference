The Universidad de los Andes consists of many buildings spread over “El eje ambiental”. In particular, the conference venue is in the “Mario Laserna” building located in Calle 19A # 1 - 82 Este (Bogota), this place is also known as the “Engineering” building. You can reach this place by taxi or public transport.

By taxi

Please enquire about certificated/secure taxis in your hotel. The cost of a taxi ride from the official hotels to the venue is around 5 USD.

By public transport

The Transmilenio bus stations closer to the venue are “Universidades” and “Las Aguas”. To see how to get the venue from these Transmilenio stations by foot, please zoom in the map below. This map also contains other important locations such as the gala dinner venue and the official hotels.
