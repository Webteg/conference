Objective:
Contact companies with young talents.



Functioning:

  • The company proposes a real challenge.

  • Teams of 4 members are formed.

  • The registration to CIbSE grants the right to participate in the Hackaton, there is no additional registration fee.

  • A group can not participate in more than one challenge.

  • Each group produces a functional solution to the challenge.

  • Groups are offered a space with tables, chairs, electrical outlets and wifi to build the solution.

  • A manager of the company resolves concerns of the challenge.

  • Particular hardware / software infrastructure is assumed by the company.

  • The best group of each challenge receives a prize.


Dates:

  • Registration: February 26 to April 24, 2018.

  • Publication of the challenges and formation of groups: April 25, 2018.

  • Closing and awarding of the hackathon: April 27, 2018, at noon.
