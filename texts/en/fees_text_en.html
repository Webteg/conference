All the articles presented in Cibse must have an associated inscription in the category "Conference (or Full) - General Public" for the deadline of the camera-ready.
(1) Includes keynotes, research tracks, industry presentations, doctoral symposium (formal invitation from the commitee is required), coffee breaks, welcome kit (bag, booklet, pen), attendance certificate, and conference proceedings in digital format.
(2) Includes tickets for Wednesday and Thursday lunches, reception cocktail and gala dinner tickets, Colombian souvenir, ticket for the closing ceremony with Colombian food and drinks on Friday.
(3) Includes article in proceedings.
(4) Includes EIbAIS tutorials, coffee breaks, school kit (sheets of paper and pen), attendance certificate.
(5) The payment for additional article will be applied to the 2nd and 3rd article to be registered by the same author and only allows the inclusion of the article in the conference proceedings but not extra printed material, food, or tickets.
(6) Students, Academic personnel and Software industry personnel must prove this condition.
If you come on behalf of an institution member of CLEI or the Colombian Computer Society (SCo2) or if you are a member of ACIS or SiSAndes, you can apply for 10% discount in your registration.
You must prove your link with the associations. Even if a given participant has multiple memberships to the mentioned associations, the discount can not exceed 10%.
