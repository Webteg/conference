The fees are shown in two currencies: USD and Colombian Pesos (COP).

In the columns header, there exist numbers associated to each category. The meaning of each number is explained at the foot of the table.

Independently of the aforementioned categories, if you come on behalf of an institution member CLEI or the Colombian Computer Society (SCo2) or if you are a member of ACIS or SiSAndes, you can apply for 10% discount in your registration.

You must prove your link with the associations (e.g., through a badge) at the registration desk the first day of your attendance to the event. Even if a given participant has multiple memberships to the mentioned associations, the discount can not exceed 10%.
