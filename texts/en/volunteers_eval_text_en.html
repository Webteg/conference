Student volunteers must be enrolled in a Ph.D. or Master program at the time of the conference and be fluent in Spanish or Portuguese.


Student volunteers must submit the online form with its accompanying documents. In addition, their advisors have to directly send a recommendation letter to the Student Volunteers Chair by email. Both submission form and CV by the applicant and recommendation letters by advisors, have to be sent no later than the 5th of March, AOE.

Around 12 student volunteer positions are available in total: we expect to assign 6 to Students from Colombian Universities and 6 to Students from the rest of Ibero-America. The selection will be made by a committee comprising members from various institutions and countries.


Selection will be based on merits and needs:
• Merit-based positions are for students with papers accepted to be presented at the conference.
• Need-based positions are for students that wish to participate in CIBSE without an accepted paper: in this case the CV of the student as well as the recommendation letter from a Professor will be the decisive factors.


Selected students will be notified no later than March 12, 2018.
