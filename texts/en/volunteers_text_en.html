The objective of this option is to offer financial aid to students who want to attend the conference

The help consists of paying the registration fees. The rest of expenses (i.e., hotels, travel expenses) must be covered by the student and / or the University that he/she represents

Candidates must apply either to a merit scholarship or a need-based scholarship:

  • By merit, it applies to students whose articles were accepted, we will review the quality of the articles and we will choose the best ones.
  • By need, it applies to students who want to come as participants, evaluate the HV, the budget and the reference of a teacher.

In both cases, to apply for the scholarship, students must fill out a form (TBD), attach a one-page HV, a teacher's reference letter and budget.
