All papers should be summited via EasyChair in the following link EasyChair . In the web page that will be deployed you will be given a list of options where you will choose one depending on the following criteria:

  • If your article falls in the category of "Technical Papers" or "Emerging Ideas Papers" you have to choose ether: Software Engineering Track, Experimental Software Engineering Track or Requirements Engineering Track.
  • If your article falls in the category of "Tools papers" please choose this option from the list.

You should submit your paper as a PDF and following the LNCS standards. At least one of the authors of every accepted paper should be register (Please check fees and payment process ) before sending the camera-ready version. For the first accepted article the registration costs are those of the category "Public in general", in case of having a second (and third) accepted article the costs are those of the category "Additional article". In addition, at least one of the paper's authors must come to the conference and present it. Otherwise, the paper will be excluded from the proceedings that are made available online by Curran Associates , and later indexed by Scopus.
