This track is the evolution of the Experimental Software Engineering Latin American Workshop, which started in 2003 in Brazil and next joined CIbSE in 2012. ESELAW provides a stimulating forum at which researchers and practitioners can present and discuss recent research results on a wide range of topics related to acquiring evidence and doing experimentation in Software Engineering. In addition, the track encourages the exchange of ideas so as to understand the strengths and weaknesses of software engineering technologies and methods from a strong evidence based viewpoint, including different kinds of empirical studies. Research on empirical/experimental methods and the design and analysis of different types of primary or secondary studies in the field of software engineering are also welcome.
