Arrangements have been made with various hotels in the different price ranges in a district of Bogota called the Financial District (also called Rosales or the Zona G). This district is approximately centered around the intersection of Calle 70 and Carrera 7. It is important to note that the Gala Dinner will be in this district.

Another option is CityU which is the provider that manages the university dorm rooms. They offer very advantageous options in the case that you come as a group.

Please check the hotels below to figure out the reservation mechanism.

We suggest to book the hotel by the time of early-bird registration for the conference because Bogota hosts other popular events such as “Feria Internacional del Libro de Bogotá” by the same dates of Cibse2018 and rooms will be going fast.

The prices below are approximate, shown in USD currency and do not include taxes.
