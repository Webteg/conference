The increasing complexity in organizations has had an enormous impact on the information systems that support their operational and strategical processes. As a result, the field has seen the emergence of new approaches, methodologies, technologies, tools and languages to describe, analyze, build, and manage such increasingly complicated systems. The goal of this track is to present and discuss theoretical and practical proposals to address the aforementioned challenges.

The following is a non exhaustive list of topics of relevance for the track:

  • Software and solution architectures
  • Enterprise Architecture, Business Architecture, and Enterprise Information Systems
  • Information Architecture
  • Infrastructure Architecture
  • Software components for the integration of Information Systems
  • Model Driven Architectures
  • Service Oriented Architectures
  • Process Oriented Architectures and Components for Process Execution
  • Agile Methodologies for Architectural Projects
  • Modeling, analysis, evaluation, simulation and automation of business processes
  • Simulation and automated analysis of architecture
  • Project and technology service management, risks and performance
