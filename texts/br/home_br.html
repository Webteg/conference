What is CCC?

The Colombian Computing Congress (CCC) is an annual gathering organized by the Colombian Computing Society (SCo2). The goal of CCC is to foster the growth of Colombia's computing community by getting together national and international researchers, students and practitioners of this discipline. The central event of the conference is the presentation of works with (i) significant research contributions (research papers) or (ii) discussions and analysis of experiences regarding innovations in different areas of computing. The CCC also includes keynotes, forums, tutorials, workshops, and a symposium for master and doctorate students.

The 10th Colombian Computing Congress (10CCC) will be hosted in Bogot치, Colombia, between the 21st and the 25th of September, 2015. In this occasion the congress will be organized by the Colombian Computing Society, the Universidad de los Andes, and IEEE Colombia. In this year's venue, we expect to increase the number of international researchers participating in the congress.
